How RAG Works

Our proven 4-step process for building intelligent systems

1

Data Ingestion

We connect to your data sources (documents, databases, APIs) and process them for semantic search. Supports PDFs, markdown, SQL, APIs, and more.

2

Vector Embeddings

Content is converted into high-dimensional vectors that capture semantic meaning, then stored in a vector database for lightning-fast retrieval.

3

LLM Integration

When users ask questions, we retrieve relevant context and provide it to the LLM, ensuring responses are grounded in your actual data.

4

Deployment & Optimization

We deploy to production with monitoring, feedback loops, and continuous improvement to maintain accuracy over time.
